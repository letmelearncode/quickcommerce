QuickCommerce Review & Rating Module PRD
====================================

1. Module Scope
--------------
The Review & Rating module manages customer feedback and product evaluations:
- Product reviews
- Seller ratings
- Customer feedback
- Review moderation
- Rating analytics
- Media attachments
- Review rewards
- Response management
- Sentiment analysis
- Quality scoring

2. Core APIs
-----------
a) Review Management
   POST /api/v1/reviews
   GET /api/v1/reviews/{id}
   PUT /api/v1/reviews/{id}
   DELETE /api/v1/reviews/{id}
   GET /api/v1/reviews/product/{productId}

b) Rating Operations
   POST /api/v1/ratings
   GET /api/v1/ratings/product/{productId}
   GET /api/v1/ratings/seller/{sellerId}
   GET /api/v1/ratings/summary/{entityId}
   PUT /api/v1/ratings/{id}

c) Moderation
   POST /api/v1/reviews/moderate
   PUT /api/v1/reviews/{id}/status
   GET /api/v1/reviews/pending
   POST /api/v1/reviews/{id}/report
   POST /api/v1/reviews/{id}/approve

d) Media Management
   POST /api/v1/reviews/{id}/media
   DELETE /api/v1/reviews/{id}/media/{mediaId}
   GET /api/v1/reviews/{id}/media
   POST /api/v1/reviews/media/moderate
