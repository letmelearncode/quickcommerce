QuickCommerce Inventory Module PRD
==============================

1. Module Scope
--------------
The Inventory module manages all aspects of product stock management in the QuickCommerce platform:
- Real-time inventory tracking
- Stock level management
- Warehouse management
- Inventory reservations
- Stock movement tracking
- Inventory alerts
- Multi-location support
- Vendor inventory integration

2. Core APIs
-----------
a) Inventory Management
   GET /api/v1/inventory/products/{productId}
   PUT /api/v1/inventory/products/{productId}/stock
   POST /api/v1/inventory/products/bulk-update
   GET /api/v1/inventory/products/low-stock

b) Stock Reservations
   POST /api/v1/inventory/reserve
   DELETE /api/v1/inventory/release
   GET /api/v1/inventory/reservations
   PUT /api/v1/inventory/reservations/{id}

c) Warehouse Operations
   GET /api/v1/inventory/warehouses
   POST /api/v1/inventory/warehouses
   GET /api/v1/inventory/warehouses/{id}/stock
   POST /api/v1/inventory/transfer

d) Inventory Analytics
   GET /api/v1/inventory/analytics/turnover
   GET /api/v1/inventory/analytics/aging
   GET /api/v1/inventory/analytics/forecast
   GET /api/v1/inventory/analytics/movement
